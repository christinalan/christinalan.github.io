This website can be a solo or group experience, done on your own time or live with others. You are testing the standalone version, which simulates what could happen during a live session. In response to Space 10's call for pieces that prioritize mental health, this website is a stand-in for channels of release and connection many of us have lost, as well as an interruption to increased, habitual digital consumption. It pulls from underground club spaces in its attempts to emulate a realm of temporality and liminality. It is a journey hinged on anticipation, stillness, and auditory and tactile exploration. Before you enter, you are given an avatar in the shape of a sonic waveform. While this simulated version will not carry the frequency you choose in the following website, you will be given a frequency that acts as your avatar. As you move through the experience, other bodies (avatars) will emerge, mimicking the experience of a communal, live event.

This experience is best with headphones and fully utilizes the affordances of a touchpad or mouse.The journey continues as one scrolls downwards and layers are revealed through visual prompts. Please 1) Click buttons as they appear 2) Hover your mouse over new visual stimuli. Audio and visual cues will guide you throughout. Lastly, please scroll SLOWLY and take your time.
