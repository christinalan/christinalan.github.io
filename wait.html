Instructions

The version you are testing simulates the live version, where your presence becomes interwoven with others who have joined the room. Your presence takes the form of an audio frequency. In the live version, you are given the chance to choose your frequency avatar by picking from this page's audio clock. In this solo version, you will be given a random frequency in the shape of a squiggly line to represent "you." Throughout the experience, more frequencies will enter the room, mimicking our eventual awareness of others who inhabit a shared space despite our initial solitude.

  • Please use headphones. This is a sonically-driven experience. Pause and listen intently to each sound that emerges.

  • Turn off the lights.

  • Click buttons as they appear. Hover your mouse over new visual stimuli. Audio and visual cues will guide you throughout.

  • Expand this window to full-screen.

  • SCROLL SLOWLY & TAKE YOUR TIME! The experience will last up to 5 minutes.

    About

    Welcome to nonclub. It is a room in passing, created in part due to the pandemic's impact on spaces that have been essential to the mental health of many. The loss of underground clubs, particularly those that evoke a realm of temporal and liminal possibility, has removed critical channels of release and transformative experimentation.

    This piece has two versions: solo and live, but currently only works as a solo experience. Neither experience attempts to replicate the experience of going to an underground club. Instead, they serve as respites that have the potential to exist beyond the pandemic. This piece is an interruption, a break from the increased ubiquity of digital interactions and an alternative to forming new connections. In the full live version, users will also be able to upload their own recordings to generate a unique sonic gathering.
