Instructions

The version you are testing simulates the live version, where your presence becomes interwoven with others who have joined the room. Your presence takes the form of an audio frequency. In the live version, you are given the chance to choose your frequency avatar by picking from this page's audio clock. However, in this solo version, you will be given a random frequency that resembles a squiggly line to represent "you." As you journey through, other frequencies will enter the room, mimicking our eventual awareness of others who inhabit a shared space despite our initial solitude.

1. Please use headphones. Direct your mobile to this URL and start the background audio. It does not need to be fully audible throughout. Place your phone behind you or somewhere not beside you (skip if you're not alone).

2. Click buttons as they appear.

3. Hover your mouse over new visual stimuli. Audio and visual cues will guide you throughout. Listen intently to the various sounds.

4. PLEASE SCROLL SLOWLY & TAKE YOUR TIME! The experience will last up to 5 minutes.

About

Welcome to nonclub. It is a room in passing, created in part due to the pandemic's impact on spaces that have been essential to the mental health of many. The loss of underground clubs, those that evoke a realm of temporal and liminal possibility, has removed critical channels of undergoing release and transformative experimentation.

This piece has two versions: solo and live, but currently only works as a solo experience. Neither attempt to replicate the experience of going to an underground club but instead, serve as respites that have the potential to exist beyond the pandemic. This piece is an interruption, a break from the increased ubiquity of digital interactions and an alternative to forming new connections. In the full live version, users will also be able to upload their own recordings to generate a unique sonic gathering.
