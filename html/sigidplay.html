SIGID PLAY

Hello, you are about to enter a virtual radio plaza.

In this world, sonic artists who primarily work with radio will stream their signal transmissions and/or compositions into the space.
As a listener participant, you are encouraged to navigate the landscape, where info about the artist's broadcast location, signals captured, and equipment used will be displayed.

The animations are audio-reactive and respond differently according to frequency and arbitrary user interaction. This platform is intended for those unfamiliar with software-defined radio and electromagnetic fields to cultivate an interest in exploring radio. To develop signal sense is to see into wireless connectivity, and to propose material cognizance of a technologically monolithic world.


*currently works in Firefox only
