Instructions

Your presence takes the form of an audio frequency. Currently, the frequency you choose from this page's audio clock will not follow you into the next room. Instead, you will be given a random frequency that resembles a squiggly line to represent "you." Throughout the experience, more frequencies will enter the room, mimicking our eventual awareness of others who inhabit a shared space despite our initial solitude.

  • Please use headphones. This is a sonically-driven experience. Pause and listen intently to each sound that emerges.

  • Turn off the lights.

  • Click buttons as they appear. Hover your mouse over new visual stimuli. Audio and visual cues will guide you throughout.

  • Expand this window to full-screen.

  • SCROLL SLOWLY & TAKE YOUR TIME! The experience will last up to 5 minutes.

    About

    Welcome to nonclub. It is a room in passing, created in part due to the pandemic's impact on spaces that have been essential to the mental health of many. The loss of underground clubs, particularly those that evoke a realm of temporal and liminal possibility, has removed critical channels of release and transformative experimentation.

    This piece does not attempt to replicate the experience of going to an underground club. Instead, it serves as a respite, one that has the potential to exist beyond the pandemic. It is an interruption, a break from the increased ubiquity of digital interactions and an alternative to forming new connections. Eventually, users will also be able to upload their own recordings to generate unique sonic narratives.
